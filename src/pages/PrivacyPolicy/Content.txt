Introduction
Welcome to Career Connect! We are dedicated to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is managed in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our placement services for college students.

We may collect the following types of information:

Educational Information: Details about your educational background, such as school/college name, degree, major, GPA, transcripts, and graduation date.
Employment Information: Information about your work experience, including internships, job titles, employers, responsibilities, and references.
Technical Information: Information collected automatically when you visit our website, including IP address, browser type, browsing activity, and cookies.
Other Information: Any other information you choose to provide, such as resumes, cover letters, and other documents relevant to your job search.
